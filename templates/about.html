About NoteGlow

Why is sharing notes so hard and slow?

That’s the question I kept asking myself, so I decided to do something about it. Introducing NoteGlow—a no-frills, simple note-sharing tool designed to help you stay productive without the hassle.

Pricing?

Free! And it will stay free for the features you use today. I do have some advanced features in mind that might cost more to host, but those are for another day when I can take a break from my consultancy work. Oh, and if you’re into self-hosting, you’re welcome to grab a copy at GitHub and host NoteGlow yourself—free forever.

Join the Community

NoteGlow is simple, and I intend to keep it that way. But that’s what makes it the perfect place to contribute! The first version was built in less than 5 hours and is easy to understand and update. Whether you’re fixing bugs, suggesting features, or improving the docs, your help can make NoteGlow even better. Let’s build something great together! NoteGlow on Github

Who Am I?

I run a software consultancy where we specialize in building websites and apps for clients. We’re a small but efficient team that handles everything, so our clients can focus on growing their business. NoteGlow started as a small project to help my team share notes quickly and efficiently. If you’re interested in having awesome software built, check us out at BaunIT.com
